Letlotlo Tshabalala, (born 17 July 1998) is a South African vocalist and aspiring music executive who is currently enrolled for a Bachelor of Music at Wits University. His vision is to change the music industry to best cater for musicians. His current focus is on the South African music industry. His current work mainly focuses on what and how external factors like social media affect music and the music industry. Since 2018, he has been in a number of South African gospel projects as a backing vocalist.
